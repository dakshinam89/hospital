Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and facilities

Appointments

You can schedule an appointment in several ways:

  • Online through our website's appointment page
  • By calling our appointment line at (123) 456-7890
  • In person at our reception desk during business hours
  • Through our mobile app (available on iOS and Android)

For urgent care needs, we offer same-day appointments based on availability.

When booking an appointment, please have the following information ready:

  • Full name and date of birth
  • Contact information (phone and email)
  • Insurance details (if applicable)
  • Reason for visit
  • Preferred date and time
  • Preferred doctor (if you have one)

For routine check-ups and non-urgent consultations, we recommend scheduling 2-3 weeks in advance to ensure you get your preferred date and time. For specialist appointments, it's advisable to book 4-6 weeks ahead as these tend to fill up quickly.

If you have an urgent medical concern, please call our office, and we will do our best to accommodate you as soon as possible, often on the same day.

We understand that plans can change. We request that you notify us at least 24 hours in advance if you need to cancel or reschedule your appointment. This allows us to offer the time slot to other patients who may need care.

Repeated no-shows or late cancellations (less than 24 hours' notice) may result in a cancellation fee of $25.

Insurance & Coverage

We accept most major insurance plans, including:

  • Blue Cross Blue Shield
  • Aetna
  • Cigna
  • UnitedHealthcare
  • Medicare
  • Medicaid

Please contact our billing department at (123) 456-7892 to verify if we accept your specific insurance plan.

We offer care to all patients regardless of insurance status. For uninsured patients, we provide:

  • Self-pay options with transparent pricing
  • Sliding fee scale based on income
  • Payment plans for larger expenses
  • Financial counseling to explore assistance programs

Our financial counselors can help you understand your options and find the most affordable way to receive care. Please contact our billing office at (123) 456-7892 for more information.

Whether you need a referral depends on your insurance plan:

  • HMO plans typically require a referral from your primary care physician
  • PPO plans generally allow you to see specialists without a referral, though your out-of-pocket costs may be higher
  • Medicare Advantage plans vary in their referral requirements

We recommend checking with your insurance provider about their referral requirements before scheduling with a specialist. If you need help navigating this process, our staff is happy to assist you.

Billing & Payments

We offer several convenient payment options:

  • Online through our patient portal
  • By phone at (123) 456-7892
  • By mail to our billing address
  • In person at our billing office

We accept cash, personal checks, and all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, and Discover).

Yes, we offer interest-free payment plans for patients who need to spread out their payments. Our financial counselors will work with you to create a plan that fits your budget.

To set up a payment plan, please contact our billing department at (123) 456-7892 or visit our billing office in person.

You may receive separate bills for different services provided during your visit. For example:

  • Hospital services (facility fees, room charges, etc.)
  • Physician services (from doctors who treated you)
  • Laboratory services (for blood tests, etc.)
  • Radiology services (for X-rays, CT scans, etc.)

If you have questions about your bills or need help understanding what you owe, please contact our billing department at (123) 456-7892.

Visiting the Hospital

Our general visiting hours are:

  • General Inpatient Units: 8:00 AM - 8:00 PM daily
  • Intensive Care Unit (ICU): 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM and 4:00 PM - 6:00 PM daily
  • Maternity Ward: 9:00 AM - 9:00 PM daily
  • Pediatric Unit: 8:00 AM - 8:00 PM (parents/guardians can stay 24/7)

Please note that visiting hours may be adjusted based on the patient's condition or special circumstances. We recommend calling ahead to confirm.

We offer several parking options:

  • Main Parking Garage: Located adjacent to the hospital entrance
  • Surface Lot A: Near the Emergency Department
  • Surface Lot B: Near the Outpatient Center

Parking rates:

  • First 30 minutes: Free
  • Up to 2 hours: $5
  • 2-4 hours: $8
  • 4-24 hours: $12
  • Weekly passes: $40

Discounted parking passes are available for patients receiving ongoing treatments and for family members of long-term patients.

Yes, we have several food options available for visitors:

  • Main Cafeteria (1st Floor): Open 6:30 AM - 8:00 PM daily
  • Coffee Shop (Main Lobby): Open 7:00 AM - 6:00 PM weekdays, 8:00 AM - 2:00 PM weekends
  • Vending Machines: Available 24/7 in various locations throughout the hospital

The cafeteria offers a variety of hot meals, sandwiches, salads, and snacks. Vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options are available.

Yes, free Wi-Fi is available throughout the hospital for patients and visitors. To connect:

  1. Select the network "MediCare-Guest" on your device
  2. Open your web browser, which will redirect to our Wi-Fi login page
  3. Accept the terms of service
  4. You'll be connected for 24 hours, after which you'll need to log in again

If you experience any issues connecting to the Wi-Fi, please ask a staff member for assistance.
